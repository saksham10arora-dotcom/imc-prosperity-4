# Round 4 - Named Counterparty Forensics

Same three products as Round 3 (HYDROGEL_PACK, VELVETFRUIT_EXTRACT, VEV Vouchers), but with two critical changes:

1. **Counterparty names were now visible.** R3 had redacted buyer/seller fields. R4 populated them with "Mark" IDs.
2. **VEV time-to-expiry dropped to 4 days** (from 5 in R3).

The research question shifted from "what kind of asset is this?" to "who exactly is on the other side of every trade?"
